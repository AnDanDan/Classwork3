Fictional Universes

Star Wars

Star Wars is a science fiction franchise set in the far distant past of our own universe. It's explored through a series of several films as well as extended media such as books, games, and other pieces of media.

Warhammer 40,000

'In the grim darkness of the far future, there is only war.' Warhammer 40,000 takes place in the 40th millenium in our own universe, chronicling humanities struggles with alien races in an attempt to stay afloat. It is primarily a table top war games played with a wide array of miniatures, but has been extensively explored through books.

Warcraft

Known as the setting of popular RTS games as well as the immensely popular MMO, Warcraft is set in a fantasy universe of swords and sorcery. It is through these games that it has been explored ane experienced by many players, though it does have books that have either added onto the story or acted as history books.

The Elder Scrolls

The Elder Scrolls is a fantasy universe that focuses primarily on the continent of Tamriel throughout various eras within that universe. It has almost exclusively been explored through a series of roleplaying games set in the universe, though there has been attempts at other game formats and other media types.

Halo

Set in the not too distant future, Halo is the name and setting of Microsoft's flagship series of video games. In this science fiction universe, humanity contends with various alien races and factions in it's bid for survival as they are threatened by devices capable of causing galactic extinctions.

Lord of the Rings

Lord of the Rings is a very popular fantasy universe explored through the works of J.R. Tolkien, and have been adapated into various movies and video games. It deals with the classic struggle of good-versus-evil.
